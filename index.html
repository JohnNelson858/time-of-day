Time of Day by John Nelson

What time is it?

Today is ...

This page uses JavaScript to determine the current hour from the client's system clock and then changes the message, image, and background color based on the time of day.

  • Morning: 5am to 12 noon
  • Afternoon: 12 noon to 6pm
  • Evening: 6pm to 11pm
  • Night: 11pm to 5am

To do this, I use the Date object and its getHours() and getDay() methods. I also use document.getElementById() and document.querySelector() to update the text, image, and page color dynamically.
